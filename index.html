My device won't cooperate

Phones, computers, tablets‚Äîthey all have moments. Let's figure out what's actually wrong before assuming the worst.

Do this first

Turn it completely off (not sleep), wait 30 seconds, turn it back on. Sounds simple, but it fixes about 40% of issues.

Common causes

Running slow? Usually too many things running, or storage is full. Both fixable.

Won't turn on? Often a battery or power issue. Sometimes just needs to charge longer than you'd expect.

Acting strange? Could be an update that went sideways, or an app causing problems.

I'm worried about scams or hacking

Take a breath. Most "hacks" are actually phishing scams or false alarms. Let's figure out what's real.

Do this first

Don't click anything else, don't call any numbers on your screen, and don't give anyone remote access. If someone called you about a virus, hang up‚Äîit's a scam.

Signs it might be a scam (not a real hack)

Pop-up with a phone number? That's a scam. Real security warnings don't include phone numbers.

Someone called you? Microsoft, Apple, and Google never call you first. Ever.

Urgency and fear? Scammers create panic on purpose. Real problems don't have countdown timers.

Asking for gift cards or wire transfers? 100% scam. No legitimate company accepts payment this way.

If you already gave access or money

Don't feel embarrassed. These scams are sophisticated and happen to smart people every day.

Contact your bank immediately if you sent money or shared financial information.

Call me. I can help assess the damage and secure your accounts.
