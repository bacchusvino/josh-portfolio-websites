Terry Weber

Leadership & Transformation Portfolio

About the Project

This section documents the planning and development of a future executive portfolio site for Terry Weber, including AI integration, blog structure, and public positioning.

Terry Weber is a transformational CEO with deep expertise in healthcare, consumer retail, and founder-led company scaling. His career spans leadership roles at organizations including Biote Medical, Amen Clinics, and Frederick's of Hollywood.

The portfolio site features a corporate-clean design with navy and teal color scheme, a blog system called "The Weber Brief," press and media coverage, board resume, and professional contact functionality.

Project Features

  • Executive landing page with impact metrics and career timeline
  • Blog system with category filtering and markdown support
  • Press & media coverage archive
  • Board resume with downloadable PDF
  • Professional contact form with inquiry types
  • Mobile-responsive, accessibility-compliant design
