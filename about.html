About

Hi, I'm Joscha.

I've spent 23 years helping real people feel less overwhelmed by technology. Not because I love gadgets (though I do), but because I hate seeing people feel stupid about something that's genuinely confusing.

Technology changes fast. Companies make things complicated on purpose. And somehow, you're supposed to figure it all out while also living your life. That's where I come in.

How I ended up doing this

I started helping people with computers when I was a teenager—mostly because I was the only one in my family who wasn't afraid to click things and see what happened. Turns out, that willingness to experiment (and the patience to explain what I learned) was valuable.

Over the years, I worked in IT for businesses, helped friends and neighbors with their home setups, and eventually realized something: the people who needed help most weren't getting it. Seniors were being talked down to. Small business owners were being sold expensive solutions to simple problems. Normal people were being made to feel stupid for not understanding things that genuinely aren't intuitive.

So I made that my focus. Patient, honest help for people who don't want to become tech experts—they just want their stuff to work.

What I believe

Technology should serve you, not the other way around. If a device or app is making your life harder, that's a problem with the technology—not with you.

There are no stupid questions. If you're confused, the explanation wasn't good enough. That's on me to fix, not on you to already know.

You don't need to understand everything. You need to understand enough to make decisions that work for your life. I'm here to help you reach that point—not to turn you into a tech person unless you want to be one.

Honest guidance matters more than sales. Sometimes the right answer is "don't buy that" or "the free version is fine" or "you don't actually need this." I'll tell you that, even though it doesn't make me money.

Where I work

I live in Idyllwild, California and serve the mountain communities nearby—Pine Cove, Mountain Center, Anza, and surrounding areas. I come to you. Most issues can be resolved in a single visit at your home or place of business.

When you call, you get me. Not a receptionist, not a call center, not a chatbot. Just a real person who will listen to what's going on and help you figure out next steps.

What you can expect

Patience

I'll explain things as many times as needed, in as many different ways as it takes. No sighing, no rushing.

Plain English

No jargon, no acronyms without explanation, no making you feel like you should already know this.

Respect

You're not dumb for not understanding technology. You've been busy living your life. That's as it should be.

Ready to get some help?

Call, text, or send a message. Describe what's going on, and I'll help you figure out next steps.
