How Remote Support Works

Remote support means I can see your screen and fix things without coming to your house. Here's exactly what happens:

  • 1. I send you a secure one-time access link. You click it, and that's the only way I can see your screen.
  • 2. You watch everything I do. My mouse movements, what I click on, all of it. No secrets.
  • 3. You can end the session at any time. Just close the window. I'm immediately disconnected.
  • 4. No software stays installed afterward. When we're done, there's no trace of the remote session on your computer.
