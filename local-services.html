About Our Mountain Communities

The Idyllwild area is a beautiful rural mountain community nestled in the San Jacinto Mountains. Unlike typical suburban neighborhoods, our mountain communities have a unique patchwork of different utility providers depending on your exact location.

The four main areas ‚Äî Idyllwild, Pine Cove, Fern Valley, and Mountain Center/Garner Valley ‚Äî each have different providers for internet, power, and water. This guide will help you understand which services are available in your specific area and how to get connected.

High-speed internet options vary by location. Check availability at your specific address.

Frontier Fiber

Fiber Optic Internet

Idyllwild Parts of Pine Cove Parts of Fern Valley

Frontier has been expanding true fiber optic service throughout the Idyllwild area. Fiber provides the fastest and most reliable internet connection available on the mountain ‚Äî ideal for streaming, video calls, home offices, and multiple devices.

  • True fiber optic connection (not DSL)
  • Expanding coverage area
  • Best option for streaming and video calls
  • Availability varies street-to-street

Spectrum

Cable Internet

Idyllwild Pine Cove

Spectrum provides cable internet service to parts of Idyllwild and Pine Cove. Cable internet is often available in areas where fiber hasn't reached yet, making it a good alternative option.

  • Cable broadband connection
  • Speeds depend on neighborhood infrastructure
  • Often available where Frontier fiber is not
  • TV and phone bundles available
Check Spectrum Availability

Anza Electric Cooperative Fiber

Fiber Optic Internet (AEC Fiber)

Mountain Center Garner Valley

Anza Electric Cooperative offers fiber internet service to customers in the Mountain Center and Garner Valley areas (ZIP 92561). This is true fiber with excellent consistency and speeds. Service is only available to customers who receive their electricity from AEC.

  • True fiber optic connection
  • Excellent reliability and consistency
  • Available only to AEC electric customers
  • Local cooperative with local support

Mail & Package Delivery

üìÆ Idyllwild Area (92549)

There is no street delivery for USPS mail in Idyllwild, Pine Cove, or Fern Valley. Residents must obtain a PO Box at the Idyllwild Post Office. UPS and FedEx may deliver to your home depending on road access ‚Äî check with each carrier.

üè† Garner Valley (92561)

Mountain Center and Garner Valley (ZIP 92561) do have street delivery for USPS mail. UPS and FedEx also deliver normally to these areas.
